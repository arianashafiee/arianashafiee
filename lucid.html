Lucid

About Lucid

Lucid is a conceptualized hardware-software system that allows users to take full control of their dreams. The product consists of the Lucid mobile app paired with the Lucid Pod device. The product allows users to extend their days into the night, enabling them to stay productive by doing a wide range of activities while also getting a good nightâ€™s rest. Some activities include studying, practicing sports, and learning rare skills like how to be a pilot.

My Role

I was the sole designer of Lucid. I brainstormed and designed the conceptualized product. All high-fidelity mockups were created in AdobeXD while the product video was produced in Final Cut Pro X and Adobe After Effects.
