About Soccer Legend

Soccer legend is a retro and soccer themed game played by hundreds of users worldwide. Players outmaneuver falling soccer balls of various sizes while collecting tokens and power-ups to increase their score. Players are encouraged to develop new strategies to break their high scores, win acheivements, and climb the global leaderboard.

My Role & Development Details

I was the sole developer of Soccer Legend. I brainstormed, designed, developed, and marketed the app. Soccer Legend was developed using the Unity Game Engine with C# scripting. All assets were either from the open domain or designed in Adobe Photoshop.
