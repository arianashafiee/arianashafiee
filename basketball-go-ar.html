About Basketball Go AR

Basketball Go AR is an augmented reality game played by hundreds worldwide where users can shoot hoops wherever they desire. Players simply start by placing a virtual basketball hoop on a computer-detected flat surface. Players can collect golden balls through scoring points and use them to purchase new types of balls. They can also climb global leaderboards by making the furthest basket, scoring the most baskets in 60 seconds, and scoring the most baskets in total.

I was the sole developer of Basketball Go AR. I brainstormed, designed, programmed (C# scripting in Unity), and marketed the app. Read more about my end-to-end development process below.
